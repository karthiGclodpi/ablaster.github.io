Hard Water:

           Water is a valuable resource on earth which needs to be managed and used properly and efficiently. Due to climatic change, water becomes hard in nature. This hardness in water forms scale in pipelines, filter, gate valves, drip irrigation pipes etc.,

 

To overcome these problem, after many years of research, we have found an affordable solution that need no chemicals and no maintenance which is called ABLASTER water conditioner.

 

ABLASTER protects from acidity and hardness of water. It helps in splitting and breaking of lime scale in pipes. So the water becomes softer and sweeter. This softened water can be used in Industries/Commercials, Agriculture and Residences.
